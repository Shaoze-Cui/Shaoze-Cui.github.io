About me

I received the B.S. degree from the China University of Geosciences (Wuhan), in 2016; the M.S. degree from Dalian University of Technology, in 2019; and the Ph.D degree from Dalian University of Technology, in 2023. Now, I am a post-doctoral with the School of Management and Economics, Beijing Institute of Technology, China. My main research interests include Healthcare Analytics, Business Analytics and Machine Learning. I focus on using AI to solve practical problems.
